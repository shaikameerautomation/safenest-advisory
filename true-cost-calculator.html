What this calculator accounts for.

1. Stage-wise Pre-EMI Interest

Banks disburse home loans in stages — not all at once. Construction-linked plans use an 8-stage curve (10% booking → 100% possession). Time-linked plans use a 7-stage curve. Interest is computed monthly on the cumulative disbursed balance at each stage. For Subvention schemes, pre-EMI is zero — but the calculator expects you to input the inflated PSF (typically 8-12% higher) so the true economics still surface.

2. GST Differential (5%)

Under-construction properties attract 5% GST without input tax credit. OC-received properties are GST-exempt. On a ₹1 Cr property, that's a ₹5L difference straight to acquisition cost.

3. Telangana Stamp Duty (7.5%)

Telangana charges 5.5% stamp duty + 1.5% transfer duty + 0.5% registration fee = 7.5% total. Applied to base agreement value at booking.

4. Rental Income — Gained or Foregone

A ready-to-move buyer earns rent from month 1. An under-construction buyer earns nothing until possession. Computed month-by-month with annual compounding (default 5%) over 48 months. Often the single largest hidden cost — ₹10-15L on a typical comparison.

5. Maintenance Charges Post-Possession

Monthly maintenance (₹3-5/sft) begins at possession. Ready-to-move buyers pay 48 months. Buyers waiting 36 months pay only 12. Partially offsets the rental advantage.

6. Luxury Index™ Normalization (Proprietary)

SafeNest's proprietary Luxury Index (0-100) measures construction quality, density, amenities, spatial design, and infrastructure. When two projects have different scores, the calculator adjusts the lower-quality project's price upward to enable like-to-like comparison. Adjustment is linear, capped at ±15%. Cross-segment comparisons trigger amber (1 band) or red (2+ bands) warnings.

7. True Cost Per Square Foot

Final output converts everything back to a per-sft number. When ₹7,000 quoted becomes ₹9,200 true and ₹9,000 quoted becomes ₹8,400 true, the conversation changes.
