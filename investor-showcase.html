Part Two · The Solution

An independent rating agency,
built for the buyer alone.

SafeNest is a RERA-registered, data-driven residential real estate rating agency. We represent buyers exclusively. We score projects using a proprietary 8-parameter model. We publish credit ratings from AAA to B. And we structure our economics so that buyer outcomes and SafeNest outcomes are permanently aligned.

01 / FOUR

Structurally Independent

SafeNest earns the same commission regardless of which project the buyer selects. Our advisory is genuinely unbiased because no builder can pay for a higher rating. This neutrality is a feature of the business model, not a promise.

02 / FOUR

Proprietary Scoring

Every project is scored on 104 parameters across legal, financial, construction, location, product, JV compliance, and demand dimensions. Output is a single AAA-to-B rating plus a proprietary Luxury Index on the 100-point scale.

03 / FOUR

AI-Powered Operations

Scoring analysis, legal document review, and report generation run on Claude. Data scraping on Apify. Workflow automation on n8n. A team of one operates at the capacity of a team of twelve.

04 / FOUR

Published Ratings

Scores are published to a public Score Directory. Buyers, brokers, developers, and journalists can reference them. Over time, the directory becomes the authoritative source of truth for Hyderabad residential projects — then beyond.

Part Five · Revenue Model

Three revenue streams.
One aligned incentive.

Every revenue line is designed to preserve buyer-side independence. The commission structure is flat across developers. The paid report is optional. The subscription is to channel partners, not buyers. SafeNest's upside aligns with buyer outcomes — not developer ones.

01 / THREE

Advisory Commission

Flat commission earned at deal closure — same rate regardless of which project the buyer selects. This neutrality is the foundation of SafeNest's independence claim.

Primary revenue · ~85% of current mix
02 / THREE

Paid Due Diligence Report

Buyers can purchase the full Deep Research Report for ₹4,999. Positioned as an optional premium; most buyers access it free via advisor path.

Monetized through Razorpay
03 / THREE

Data-as-a-Service

Annual subscriptions for channel partners — brokers, wealth managers, NRI advisors — who want access to SafeNest ratings for their own clients.

Enterprise SaaS model

We earn the same commission regardless of which project the buyer chooses. That's not a marketing line. That's the architecture of our business — and the reason our ratings can be trusted when no other rating in Indian real estate can.
