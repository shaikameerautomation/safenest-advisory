What this calculator accounts for.

1. The OTP Discount

Builders offer 6-12% off base price for upfront payment. The calculator treats this as a real saving — but compares it against what the buyer's capital could earn elsewhere.

2. Opportunity Cost of Capital (The Critical Variable)

When a buyer pays ₹1 Cr upfront under OTP, that ₹1 Cr is no longer earning anywhere else. A salaried professional in a debt fund earns ~6%. A balanced investor earns ~8%. A business owner deploying capital in inventory may earn 12%+. The calculator computes the opportunity cost on the full upfront payment over the handover period — and compares it to the discount.

3. CLP Opportunity Gain (The Hidden Win)

Under CLP, the buyer's own contribution stays in their hands and continues to earn returns until each stage payment is due. The calculator computes this opportunity gain stage-by-stage using the same disbursement curve as the loan — but applied to the buyer's own equity, not the bank's loan.

4. Pre-EMI Interest (CLP Cost)

Under CLP, the buyer pays interest on the bank's disbursed loan amount each month before possession. This is computed on a stage-wise basis using the standard 8-stage construction-linked curve.

5. Section 24 Tax Savings (CLP Win)

Pre-construction interest can be claimed in 5 equal installments after possession, capped at ₹2L per year. For a 30% tax bracket buyer, this can return ₹3L+ over 5 years. OTP buyers lose this benefit entirely because there is no interest to claim.

6. Break-Even Analysis

The calculator surfaces two break-even points: (a) the discount % at which OTP would exactly equal CLP for this buyer's specific opportunity cost, and (b) the opportunity cost rate at which CLP would exactly equal OTP at the current discount. Both are decision-grade insights.

7. Risk-Adjusted Flag

When the math says OTP wins but the developer is BB or B rated under SafeNest scoring, the calculator flags the risk. Paying ₹1 Cr upfront to a high-risk developer is reckless regardless of mathematical advantage. The advisor delivers the recommendation — the calculator delivers the warning.

8. Advisor Negotiation Mode (Internal Only)

When toggled on, the calculator shows the discount % the advisor should target to make OTP win by a comfortable 3% margin. This is ammunition for negotiating with the builder on behalf of the buyer. Never shown to the buyer directly.
